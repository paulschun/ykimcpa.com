Originally founded in San Rafael, Young Kim Accounting Solutions has, for over twenty-five years, served residents of Marin County (in addition to clients from around the globe) with their business and personal accounting and taxation needs. A former long-term resident of Marin herself before moving into the city, Young has a soft spot for all things Marin. Her two children were educated in schools in the Miller Creek School District and San Rafael City Schools districts, and she enjoyed the hilly terrain that Marin has to offer, but the years of commuting into the city on the congested Southbound 101 became too much to bear.
